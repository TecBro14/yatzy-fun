    Scoring Guide

    1s to 6s: Sum of only the dice matching that number. (Ex: three 5s = 15 pts)

    3K / 4K: Sum of all dice if at least 3 or 4 match.

    FH (Full House): 3 of one kind + 2 of another. (25 pts)

    SS (Small Straight): 4 dice in sequence (e.g., 1-2-3-4). (30 pts)

    LS (Large Straight): 5 dice in sequence (e.g., 1-2-3-4-5). (40 pts)

    Yatzy: 5 of a kind. (50 pts)

    Chance: Sum of all 5 dice regardless of what they are.
